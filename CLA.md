## How to sign

When you open a pull request, add the following single line to the PR
description, replacing the fields with your details:

```

```

That's it. No paperwork, no printed signatures. By including that line
you accept the terms above for every Contribution in that PR and for
any future Contributions you make to the project.

If you are contributing on behalf of your employer, use the employer's
name after "Signed:".
